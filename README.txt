Pokemon FlameRed is a rom hack of Pokemon Fire Red. FlameRed is a more difficult and diverse 
version of Fire Red with more Pokemon, different maps, harder trainers, etc. The story line is
still the same, giving the same feel as the original Fire Red.

Pokemon Orang (too large for GitHub) is a rom hack of Pokemon Emerald. The goal behind Orang is to have the player start with 
many lvl. 100 pokemon and progress through very difficult battles right from the start. Orang will be 
a much shorter, much different game than Emerald.
